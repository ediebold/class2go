Which of the following strings doesn't match the regex: 

/[a-z]+'[a-z]+/

randFromArray(["you'd've", "We'd", "rock 'n' roll", "'tis", "I'm"])
ANS
  • he'd
  • it's
  • cat's
  • isn't
  • won't
  • oughtn't
  • shan't
  • mightn't
  • we're

Answers are matched when they are all lower-case, and contain alphabetical characters on each side of a single apostrophe.
